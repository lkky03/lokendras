About Government Polytechnic Nainital

Government Polytechnic Nainital, located in the picturesque hills of Uttarakhand, is a premier institution offering technical education in various engineering and diploma courses. Since its establishment, it has been at the forefront of producing skilled professionals who contribute significantly to the nation's technical workforce.

With a strong emphasis on practical skills, innovative thinking, and holistic development, we aim to empower our students with knowledge, confidence, and the right attitude to face real-world challenges.

Available Courses

Information Technology (IT)

This course includes programming, algorithms, data structures, and web development. You'll learn about modern software tools and databases. Ideal for students who love computers and want a career in software or tech companies.

Electronics Engineering

Learn about circuits, transistors, ICs, sensors, and microprocessors. This course is perfect for students interested in gadgets, embedded systems, and electronic devices.

Electrical Engineering

This program focuses on power systems, electrical machines, transformers, and wiring. Great for those looking to work in electrical companies or government departments.

Pharmacy

Learn about medicine preparation, lab testing, and pharmaceutical knowledge. A solid path to becoming a medical store operator or working in pharma companies or hospitals.

Civil Engineering

This branch covers construction, building design, road works, and surveying. Students learn AutoCAD and structural design. Perfect for those interested in site work and infrastructure development.
